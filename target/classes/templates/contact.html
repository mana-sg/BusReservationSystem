Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Booking a bus ticket is easy. Simply visit our website or app, select your origin and destination cities, choose your travel date, and pick a bus that suits your preferences. You can then select your seat, enter passenger details, and complete the payment to receive your e-ticket.

To cancel your ticket, log in to your account, go to 'My Bookings', select the booking you wish to cancel, and click on the 'Cancel Ticket' button. Refunds are processed based on our cancellation policy, which varies depending on how close to the departure time you cancel. Refunds typically take 5-7 business days to reflect in your account.

You can track your bus in real-time by using our mobile app. After booking, go to 'My Bookings', select your active trip, and tap on 'Track Bus'. This will show you the current location of your bus, estimated time of arrival at your boarding point, and any updates regarding your journey.

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), net banking, UPI, popular wallets like PayTM and PhonePe, and EMI options from select banks. All our payment gateways are secure and comply with the latest security standards.

Each passenger is allowed to carry one piece of luggage up to 15 kg and one handbag. Additional luggage may be subject to extra charges and availability of space. Please note that certain items like flammable materials, weapons, and perishable goods are not permitted. For special luggage requirements, please contact our customer support in advance.
