About Bus Management System

The Bus Management System is a comprehensive solution designed to streamline bus operations and enhance user experience through modern web technologies.

Key Features:

  • Fleet Management - Add, update, and track buses in your fleet
  • Route Planning - Create and optimize routes for maximum efficiency
  • Schedule Management - Create and manage bus schedules
  • Booking System - Allow passengers to book tickets online
  • Reporting - Generate reports on bus operations, bookings, etc.
